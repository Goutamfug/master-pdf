About Us

Hum multiple tools offer karte hain jaise:

  • Merge PDF (multiple files ko combine karna)
  • Split PDF (pages alag karna)
  • Compress PDF (file size kam karna)
  • JPG to PDF (images ko PDF me convert karna)
  • PDF to Word conversion
  • AI-based PDF Summary

Hamari website ka sabse bada advantage hai ki yeh 100% browser-based hai. Iska matlab hai ki aapke files kisi server par upload nahi hote — sab processing aapke device par hoti hai, jo aapki privacy ko secure banata hai.

Hum simple, fast aur secure experience dene par focus karte hain, taaki students, professionals aur daily users sab aasani se PDF tools use kar sake.
